The choosen game is: Concentration (memory game)

• Choose a theme for the game. 

• Create 16 cards with 8 matching pairs of images.

• Set the timer to 60 seconds. 

• Set the maximum number of wrong guesses. 

• Shuffle the cards randomly. 

• Flip the card to show its image. 

• If it is the second card selected 

• Check if the two cards match. 

• If the cards match mark both cards as matched and keep them visible. 

• If the cards do not match flip both cards back over. 

• Show the message: Game Over! You lost or won.
